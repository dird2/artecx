Do you replace 999 emergency services?

No. If someone‚Äôs life is in immediate danger (severe breathing problems, chest pain, heavy bleeding, unconsciousness), you must call 999. We support urgent and planned transfers where 999 is not appropriate or has advised alternatives.

Can a family member or carer travel with the patient?

In most cases, yes. Space and safety permitting, one escort can travel. For ICU or high-dependency transfers, this may not be possible and we‚Äôll explain the options.
